Highlight Events

JEH

Are you one of those who are still confused between what to pursue in their future. Jobs vs Entrepreneurship vs Higher Education is a way to resolve what to do in your future. With Every field having its own pros and cons, deciding the correct future is a very tough job for every student. Come and join us in a discussion which might help you decide what future you want to pursue

E-Summit 19

E-Summit 2019 is a two day entrepreneurial conclave that is conducted by E-Cell IIT Bombay to bring together all the sections of entrepreneurial ecosystem under a single roof. With Events catering to every aspect of entrepreneurial ecosystem, you surely want to visit India’s largest student organised entrepreneurial conclave.

Mentors Meet

Mentor Meetup was a networking event to help connecting entrepreneurs and enthusiasts to investors, mentors and market leaders all across the country. Here, attendees had one-on-one mentoring and CNT (Common Networking Time) where they interacted with different startups at the event.
